About Artisan Luthier Tools

Handcrafted precision tools for luthiers and music professionals, made with passion and expertise in Chicago.

Founded in 2024 in Chicago, United States, Artisan Luthier Tools was established with the mission of providing premium-quality tools for the repair and maintenance of string instruments. Our company offers a comprehensive range of products designed to ensure that luthiers and music professionals have the proper resources to preserve and enhance the tonal essence of violins, violas, cellos, guitars, and other string instruments.

Our products have been designed and are continuously being developed by maestro violin maker and restorer Yucel Acin, based in Chicago, and by Ecevit Tunali, based in New York. Our products are handcrafted by maestro professional metalsmith.

Every tool we create is designed and tested in our Chicago workshop, ensuring it meets the highest standards for both professional luthiers and passionate hobbyists. We believe that the right tool can elevate your craft, inspire creativity, and help you achieve the perfect sound.

Whether you're a seasoned professional or an aspiring maker, we are here to support your journey with tools you can trust.
