About Artisan Luthier Tools

Handcrafted precision tools for luthiers and music professionals, made with passion and expertise in Chicago.

Founded in 2024 in Chicago, United States, Artisan Luthier Tools was established with the mission of providing premium-quality tools for the repair and maintenance of string instruments.

Our products are designed by maestro violin maker and restorer Yucel Acin (Chicago) and Ecevit Tunali (New York), and handcrafted by a master metalsmith.

Every tool we create is designed and tested in our Chicago workshop, ensuring it meets the highest standards for both professionals and passionate hobbyists.

Whether you're a seasoned luthier or an aspiring maker, we are here to support your journey with tools you can trust.
