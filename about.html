Our Story

Crafting precision tools for the world's finest luthiers since 2024

Precision Born from Passion

Founded in 2024 in Chicago, Artisan Luthier Tools was established with a clear mission: to provide premium-quality tools for the repair and maintenance of string instruments.

Our company offers a comprehensive range of products designed to ensure that luthiers and music professionals have the proper resources to preserve and enhance the tonal essence of violins, violas, cellos, guitars, and other string instruments.

Every tool we create is designed and tested in our Chicago workshop, ensuring it meets the highest standards for both professional luthiers and passionate hobbyists. We believe that the right tool can elevate your craft, inspire creativity, and help you achieve the perfect sound.
